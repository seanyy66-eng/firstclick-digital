Cold Call Script & List

Opening Script

"Hi, is this [Contact/Owner]? My name's Sean from FirstClick Digital. I'm not trying to sell you anything today - I was actually doing some research on [industry] businesses in [location] and noticed that [Business Name] doesn't have a website / has a website that could be working harder for you. I help businesses like yours get found on Google and get booked online. Would you have 2 minutes for me to explain how we could help?"

If They Say Yes

"Great - so basically what we do is build you a professional website with an online booking system, get you ranking on Google so people in your area find you first, and we can even run some targeted ads. We handle everything - domain, email, hosting, the lot. Most of our clients see a noticeable uptick in enquiries within the first month. Would you be keen for me to send you through some info, or we could jump on a quick 10-minute call later this week to chat properly?"

If They Say No / Not Interested

"No worries at all - I completely understand. Would it be alright if I sent you a quick email with some free info on what your competitors are doing online? No obligation, just thought it might be useful. Cheers, have a good one."
